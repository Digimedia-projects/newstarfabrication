Our Services

POLYCARBON SHADE

A durable, lightweight roofing solution, polycarbonate shades provide excellent protection against UV rays while allowing natural light to pass through. Ideal for outdoor spaces like patios, carports, and terraces.

CHANNEL GATE

Channel gates are robust, sliding gates made from high-quality metal, providing security and easy access. These gates are commonly used in residential, industrial, and commercial properties for reliable protection.

RACKY NOTE

A versatile storage system, racky notes are metal racks designed to maximize space and organize items efficiently. Perfect for warehouses, garages, and industrial settings, they provide strength and durability.

BOX GRILL

Box grills are sturdy and protective metal structures commonly installed in windows and balconies, offering enhanced security while maintaining airflow and visibility. They are ideal for residential and commercial use.

WINDOW GRILL

Window grills are safety features designed to protect windows from intrusion while adding an aesthetic touch to the building. Made from strong materials, they provide both security and style to any property.

SAFETY DOOR

Safety doors are reinforced doors designed to enhance the security of homes and offices. Constructed with high-strength materials, they act as an additional barrier, safeguarding against forced entry while maintaining a sleek appearance.
